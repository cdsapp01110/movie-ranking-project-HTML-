The Best Movies According to Chastity

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Aristocats

A fun cartoon movie based in 1970s France from the perspective of cats.

The Equalizer 3

A well balanced action/suspense movie. I love the scenes from the small villages of the Amalfi Coast in Italy.

A Thousand Years of Longing

A romantic fantasy that was filmed in Sydney, Australia, and Istanbul, Turkey.
